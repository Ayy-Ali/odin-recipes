Grilled Cheese Sandwich Recipe


Description

A grilled cheese, toasted cheese sandwich, cheese toastie or cheese jaffle is a hot cheese sandwich typically prepared by placing cheese between slices of bread and cooking with butter or mayonnaise on a frying pan, griddle, or sandwich toaster, until the bread browns and the cheese melts.

Ingredients

  • Freshly Grated Cheese
  • Sourdough Bread
  • Salted Butter

Steps

  1. Butter the breads
  2. Preheat a nonstick pan
  3. Place the bread on the pan and cheese on it
  4. Place another piece of bread on top of it and flip it after a few mins
  5. Cook until brown and serve!!
